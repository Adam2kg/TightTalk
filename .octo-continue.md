## Key decisions

- **No synthetic silence anywhere** — every inserted gap uses `audio[next_start_ms - new_ms : next_start_ms]` (end of original gap), so it flows naturally into the next word
- **cut_threshold_ms = int(400 - 350 * aggression)** — at 0.3 → 295ms; gaps shorter than this are kept entirely as-is
- **pause_floor_ms = int(250 - 200 * aggression)** — floor for compressed gaps
- **protected_max_ms = int(800 - 500 * aggression)** — ceiling for island-protected pauses
- Default aggression: 0.4
- Breath mode "Replace with micro-silence" now keeps the tail of the gap, not dead silence
